## Apartment sales in Saint Petersburg - Real estate market analysis

Based on the data from a Real Estate service, the market value of real estate of different types, 
typical parameters of apartments, depending on the distance from the center were determined. 
Pre-processing of data was carried out. New data was added. 
Histograms, boxplots, and scatter diagrams were built.

__Keywords__: data processing, histogram, boxplot, scatter matrix, categorization, scatterplot, fraud monitoring.
